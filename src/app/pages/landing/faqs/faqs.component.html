FAQS

How does Sky ensure the security and privacy of my data?

Sky prioritizes your security and privacy. We use end-to-end encryption to protect your conversations and data. Additionally, we offer two-factor authentication (2FA) and advanced privacy settings so you have complete control over your information.

 Can I integrate Sky with other tools and apps?

Yes, Sky offers powerful integrations with a wide range of third-party tools and apps, including cloud storage services like Google Drive and Dropbox, productivity apps like Trello and Asana, and calendar services like Google Calendar and Outlook.

 What kind of customization options does Sky offer?

Sky provides extensive customization options, including custom themes and color schemes, configurable notifications, and custom emojis and reactions. You can tailor Sky to fit the unique needs of your team.

How can I manage my team on Sky?

Sky offers robust team management features, including custom roles and permissions, user administration, and usage analytics and reports. You can easily manage your team and ensure everyone has the appropriate access.

 What kind of support does Sky provide?

Sky provides 24/7 technical support to assist you with any issues or questions. We also offer detailed documentation, tutorials, and a user community and help forums for additional support.
